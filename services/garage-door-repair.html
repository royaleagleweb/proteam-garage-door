Garage Door Repair Services in South Florida

When your garage door stops working, you need a team that responds fast and fixes it right the first time. ProTeam Garage Door provides same-day repair across Broward and Palm Beach County, with fully stocked trucks ready to handle any issue on the spot.

Common Garage Door Problems We Fix

Broken & Damaged Panels

Cracked, dented, or broken panels compromise both appearance and security. We carry replacement panels for most major brands and can often match your existing door's color and style. When damage is too extensive, we advise honestly whether a full door replacement makes more financial sense.

Off-Track Doors & Cable Issues

A door that has jumped its tracks is a serious safety hazard and should never be forced. Our technicians carefully realign the door, inspect tracks for bends or obstructions, and replace frayed or snapped cables. We also check the drum and bottom bracket to prevent future occurrences.

Noisy Doors & Roller Replacement

Grinding, squealing, or rattling sounds indicate worn rollers, loose hardware, or lack of lubrication. We replace steel rollers with ultra-quiet nylon rollers, tighten all brackets and hinges, and apply professional-grade lubricant. The noise difference is dramatic and immediate.

Why Choose ProTeam for Repairs

Our trucks carry over 200 parts, so most jobs are completed in a single visit. We provide upfront pricing with no surprise charges. Every repair includes a multi-point safety inspection covering springs, cables, opener function, sensors, and weatherstripping.

Emergency Repair Service

Garage door emergencies do not follow business hours. We offer 24/7 emergency response when your home security is compromised or a vehicle is trapped. Our dispatchers will have a technician en route quickly with the tools and parts needed to restore your door.
