Garage Door Maintenance & Tune-Up Services

Regular maintenance extends your garage door's life and prevents unexpected breakdowns. ProTeam Garage Door offers tune-ups and maintenance plans for homeowners and businesses across Broward and Palm Beach County.

What Our Tune-Up Includes

Lubrication of All Moving Parts

South Florida's heat and humidity accelerate wear on metal parts. We apply professional-grade lubricant to rollers, hinges, bearing plates, springs, and the opener's drive mechanism. Proper lubrication reduces friction, quiets operation, and prevents corrosion.

Safety System Testing

Federal law requires every automatic door to have a functioning auto-reverse and photoelectric sensors. We test both, adjusting the opener's force settings and realigning sensors as needed. We also inspect the emergency release, check manual balance, and verify mounting hardware is secure.

Weather Seal Replacement

Bottom, side, and header seals keep rain, insects, and drafts out of your garage. Florida's UV exposure degrades rubber seals quickly. We inspect all perimeter seals and replace any that are cracked, brittle, or no longer making contact. Fresh weatherstripping improves efficiency and protects belongings from moisture.

Preventive Maintenance Plans

Our annual and semi-annual plans provide scheduled visits so you never forget a tune-up. Members receive priority scheduling, discounted parts, and a written condition report after each visit. This proactive approach catches worn cables, loose brackets, or developing rust before they become emergency repairs.

Benefits of Regular Maintenance

Well-maintained doors last significantly longer, with springs and openers often reaching twice their expected lifespan. You enjoy quieter operation, improved security, better efficiency, and peace of mind. For commercial properties, maintenance plans help satisfy insurance and code requirements while minimizing disruptions.
