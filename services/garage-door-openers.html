Garage Door Opener Installation & Repair

Your garage door opener operates over a thousand cycles per year and is the workhorse of your garage system. ProTeam Garage Door installs and repairs all types of residential openers across Broward and Palm Beach County.

Types of Garage Door Openers

Belt-Drive Openers

Belt-drive openers use a reinforced rubber belt, making them the quietest option available. Ideal for homes with living spaces above or adjacent to the garage, LiftMaster's premium line features steel-reinforced belts delivering whisper-quiet performance with zero chain maintenance.

Chain-Drive Openers

Chain-drive units are the most affordable and time-tested type, using a metal chain to lift and lower the door. Modern chain-drive openers from Chamberlain have significantly reduced vibration compared to older generations, making them a reliable budget-friendly choice.

Screw-Drive & Wall-Mount Openers

Screw-drive openers use a threaded steel rod with very few moving parts, resulting in minimal maintenance. Wall-mount (jackshaft) openers attach beside the door, freeing ceiling space entirely. These are excellent for garages with high ceilings or overhead storage needs.

Smart WiFi-Enabled Openers

Connected openers let you monitor and control your garage door from anywhere via smartphone. Receive alerts when the door opens, grant temporary access to delivery drivers, and integrate with Alexa and Google Home. LiftMaster's myQ and Chamberlain's smart platform lead the industry in connected garage solutions.

Opener Repair Services

Our technicians diagnose and repair all brands. Common issues include stripped gears, faulty logic boards, worn drive components, and sensor misalignment. We carry parts for most brands and complete repairs in a single visit. When costs approach replacement price, we recommend upgrading.

Pair your new opener with a new garage door or schedule routine maintenance to extend its lifespan. We also service commercial opener systems.
