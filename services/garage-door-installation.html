Garage Door Installation in South Florida

Replacing an aging garage door transforms both the look and functionality of your home. At ProTeam Garage Door, we specialize in residential installation throughout Broward and Palm Beach County, helping homeowners select the perfect door for their style and budget.

Premium Materials for Every Home

Steel doors remain the most popular choice for their durability, low maintenance, and excellent insulation. Aluminum doors provide a sleek, modern aesthetic and naturally resist rust in our coastal climate. Wood garage doors deliver unmatched warmth and character, while glass panel doors create a stunning contemporary look that floods your garage with natural light.

Top Brands We Install

Clopay, Amarr, Wayne Dalton & C.H.I.

We partner with the industry's most trusted manufacturers. Clopay offers the widest range of residential designs, Amarr is known for exceptional build quality, Wayne Dalton leads in innovative features, and C.H.I. delivers outstanding customization. Every door comes backed by a full manufacturer warranty plus our labor guarantee.

Energy Efficiency & Curb Appeal

A new garage door is the single best home improvement for ROI, recouping over 90% of its cost at resale. Our insulated options feature polyurethane or polystyrene cores with R-values up to 18, keeping your garage comfortable year-round. With hundreds of colors, panel styles, window inserts, and decorative hardware, your new door will boost curb appeal instantly.

Our Installation Process

Every project begins with a free on-site consultation where we measure your opening and present tailored options. Our factory-trained technicians handle everything from old door removal to final safety testing, with most installations completed in a single day.

Explore our other services including garage door repair, opener installation, spring replacement, and hurricane-rated doors.
