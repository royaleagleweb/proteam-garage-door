About ProTeam Garage Door

Family-owned and operated since 2004, we've been South Florida's go-to garage door professionals for over two decades.

Our Story

ProTeam Garage Door was founded with a simple mission: deliver honest, high-quality garage door services at fair prices. What started as a two-person operation working out of a single truck has grown into one of South Florida's most respected garage door companies, with a full team of trained technicians serving communities throughout Broward and Palm Beach County.

Every member of our crew goes through rigorous hands-on training before they ever set foot on a job site. We believe the only way to guarantee consistent quality is to invest in our people — and that philosophy has earned us thousands of five-star reviews from homeowners and businesses across the region.

What Sets Us Apart

In an industry filled with fly-by-night operators and bait-and-switch pricing, we take a different approach. We show up when we say we will, we charge what we quote, and we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. One of our owners personally oversees every major installation to ensure the finished product meets our standards — not just the building code minimum.

Our Core Values

Integrity First

We provide honest assessments and will never recommend work you don't need. If a repair can solve your problem, we won't push a full replacement.

Craftsmanship

We treat every garage door like it's going on our own home. Attention to detail, clean installations, and proper alignment are non-negotiable for our team.

Community

We live and work in the same neighborhoods we serve. Supporting local families and businesses isn't just our job — it's personal.

Responsiveness

Garage door emergencies don't wait for business hours. Our 24/7 emergency line means you're never stranded with a broken door.

Licensed, Insured & Certified

ProTeam Garage Door holds a valid Florida contractor license and maintains full general liability and workers' compensation insurance. Our technicians are factory-trained and certified by leading manufacturers including Clopay, Amarr, LiftMaster, and Chamberlain. When you hire ProTeam, you're hiring professionals who know your specific door inside and out.

Our Service Area

We proudly serve homeowners and businesses across Broward County and Palm Beach County, including Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton, West Palm Beach, Coral Springs, Pompano Beach, Hollywood, Plantation, Davie, Weston, Deerfield Beach, Delray Beach, Boynton Beach, Sunrise, Parkland, Coconut Creek, Jupiter, Palm Beach Gardens, Wellington, Margate, and Tamarac.
