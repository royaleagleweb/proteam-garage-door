Why Finance Your Garage Door?

Home Security

A quality garage door protects your family against intruders, pests, and severe weather. Don't settle for a worn-out door simply because of budget — financing lets you get the protection you need today.

Property Value

New garage doors are the #1 ROI home improvement project nationwide. Financing lets you invest now and start benefiting from increased curb appeal and resale value immediately.

Hurricane Protection

Florida homeowners need storm-ready doors to protect their property. Finance a hurricane-rated garage door and you may even save on homeowners insurance premiums.

Energy Efficiency

Insulated garage doors help reduce energy costs year-round. Modern doors with polyurethane insulation can noticeably lower your monthly utility bills in South Florida's heat.

Financing FAQ

What credit score is needed?
Most applicants with fair to good credit are approved. We encourage you to apply — there's no obligation and the inquiry is quick.

Is there a minimum purchase?
Financing is available on garage door installations of $1,000 or more.

Can I pay it off early?
Yes, absolutely. There are no prepayment penalties, so you can pay off your balance at any time without extra fees.
