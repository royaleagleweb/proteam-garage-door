Hurricane-Rated Garage Doors

Your garage door is the largest opening in your home and the first line of defense during a hurricane. A failure here can lead to catastrophic pressure changes that destroy your roof from the inside out.

Wind Load Certified

Our hurricane-rated garage doors are tested to withstand sustained winds exceeding 180 mph, satisfying the strictest Florida Building Code mandates including the High-Velocity Hurricane Zone standards required in Miami-Dade and Broward counties.

Impact Resistant

Reinforced with heavy-gauge steel bracing and impact-rated glazing, these doors guard against airborne debris — the leading cause of structural envelope failure during tropical cyclones and major hurricanes.

Insurance Premium Discounts

Florida Statute §627.0629 requires insurers to offer wind mitigation discounts. Homeowners who install approved hurricane garage doors frequently save hundreds per year on property insurance after a certified wind mitigation inspection.

Boost Your Property Value

A new garage door ranks among the highest ROI home improvements in America — up to 194% return according to national cost-versus-value studies. Hurricane-rated doors add both protection and resale appeal.
