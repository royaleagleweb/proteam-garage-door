Hurricane Season Ready

Hurricane-Rated Garage Doors

Your garage door is the largest opening in your home — and the most vulnerable during a hurricane. Protect your family and property with wind-rated garage doors.

Wind Load Rated

Our hurricane garage doors are tested and certified to withstand winds up to 180+ mph, meeting the strictest Florida Building Code requirements including HVHZ standards for Miami-Dade and Broward County.

Impact Resistant

Reinforced with heavy-duty bracing and impact-resistant materials, these doors protect against flying debris — the #1 cause of structural damage during hurricanes.

Insurance Savings

Under Florida law (Statute §627.0629), homeowners with wind-rated garage doors may qualify for significant insurance discounts. Schedule a wind mitigation inspection to see your savings.

Increase Home Value

A new garage door delivers one of the highest ROIs for home improvements — up to 194% return on investment according to Remodeling Magazine's Cost vs. Value report.
