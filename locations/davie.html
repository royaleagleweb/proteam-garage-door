Garage Door Solutions for Davie, FL Homes & Ranches

Davie proudly preserves its Western heritage while embracing modern growth. From the Bergeron Rodeo Grounds to equestrian trails winding through neighborhoods, Davie's character is unlike anywhere else in South Florida. ProTeam Garage Door understands the distinctive properties here and delivers services built around this community's needs.

Ranch properties and equestrian estates require oversized garage and barn-style doors for trailers, large vehicles, and equipment. Our team specializes in custom-width installations, heavy-duty tracks, and high-lift configurations providing the clearance these operations demand.

From Old Davie to New Developments

The historic Old Davie district features charming homes with single-car garages needing modernized systems. We replace worn wooden doors with durable steel or composite alternatives capturing the rustic aesthetic residents love, including carriage house designs with decorative strap hinges and crossbuck panels.

Near Nova Southeastern University, newer communities feature suburban homes with two-car garages. These properties benefit from insulated door installations, LiftMaster smart openers, and maintenance plans keeping everything running smoothly.

Built Tough for Davie's Storm Season

Large garage openings on ranch properties are especially vulnerable during hurricanes. We install wind-rated doors meeting Broward County codes, protecting barns, workshops, and garages from severe wind damage. Every hurricane-rated installation includes proper permitting and passes inspection.
