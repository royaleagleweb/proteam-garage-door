Oversized & Carriage House Garage Doors for Wellington, FL Estates

Wellington is internationally recognized as the winter equestrian capital, home to the prestigious International Polo Club and sprawling horse estates across western Palm Beach County. From the family-friendly Olympia area to grand equestrian properties, Wellington residences are distinguished by generous proportions and distinctive architecture. ProTeam Garage Door specializes in the oversized and specialty solutions these properties demand.

Oversized Doors for Large Estate Properties

Wellington estates frequently require garage doors beyond standard dimensions, whether for horse trailers, RVs, or specialty vehicles. ProTeam engineers custom-sized solutions with commercial-grade track systems and heavy-duty torsion springs rated for increased weight. Our installation team handles openings up to 20 feet wide and 14 feet tall, ensuring smooth daily operation.

Barn and Carriage House Style Doors

Nothing complements Wellington's equestrian character like an authentic carriage house or barn-style garage door. We offer swing-out carriage doors with strap hinges and ring pulls, or overhead carriage-style doors with automatic operation. Designs are available in knotty alder, western red cedar, reclaimed barnwood, and maintenance-free composite alternatives.

Serving Wellington's Unique Property Needs

Many Wellington properties feature multiple outbuildings with distinct door requirements. ProTeam coordinates multi-structure projects for consistent styling while selecting appropriate specifications for each building. We provide hurricane-rated options for all structures and heavy-duty opener systems with extended warranties for active equestrian estates.
