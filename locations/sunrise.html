Residential & Commercial Garage Door Services in Sunrise, FL

Sunrise has evolved into one of western Broward County's most active cities, balancing commercial corridors with established neighborhoods. Home to Sawgrass Mills and the Amerant Bank Arena, Sunrise generates strong demand for both commercial and residential garage door services that ProTeam Garage Door meets with expert craftsmanship.

Commercial properties along the Sawgrass Expressway rely on heavy-duty rolling steel doors and high-speed systems for constant use. Our commercial division installs and repairs these with minimal disruption, keeping loading docks and parking structures functional.

Sunrise Residential Garage Door Specialists

The Sunrise Lakes community and surrounding neighborhoods depend on reliable garage doors daily. Many residences built in the 1970s and 1980s have systems at end of life. We replace aging doors with energy-efficient insulated models that improve curb appeal and reduce cooling costs.

Newer subdivisions west of Nob Hill Road feature homes with two-car garages pairing well with our premium lines from Clopay, Amarr, and Wayne Dalton. We offer styles from raised-panel to contemporary designs with LiftMaster or Chamberlain smart openers.

Hurricane-Ready Solutions for Sunrise

Sunrise sits inland but remains exposed to hurricane-force winds across Broward County. We install impact-rated doors and retrofit existing systems with wind-load braces satisfying local building codes. Preparing your garage door before storm season is one of the most effective steps to protect your property.
