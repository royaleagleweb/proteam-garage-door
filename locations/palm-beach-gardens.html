Resort-Quality Garage Door Installations in Palm Beach Gardens, FL

Palm Beach Gardens embodies the best of South Florida resort-style living, from the world-famous PGA National Resort to upscale shopping at The Gardens Mall and lively Downtown at the Gardens. This Palm Beach County city attracts discerning homeowners who appreciate refined aesthetics. ProTeam Garage Door delivers installations that meet the elevated expectations of this community.

Mediterranean and Modern Styles for Golf Communities

Palm Beach Gardens is defined by stunning golf course communities with Mediterranean villas alongside sleek modern residences. Our Mediterranean collection features raised panel doors with decorative iron hardware and arched window inserts. For contemporary homes, we offer flush-panel aluminum and glass doors with clean lines and minimalist hardware. Every installation is precision-fitted to complement your home's architectural character.

Premium Materials and Expert Craftsmanship

We partner with top manufacturers including Clopay, Amarr, and C.H.I. to offer triple-layer insulated steel, genuine wood overlays, and architectural aluminum. Our factory-trained installers ensure perfect alignment and a flawless appearance. We pair every door with premium opener systems featuring battery backup and smartphone integration.

Protecting Your Investment

ProTeam offers tailored maintenance programs with seasonal inspections, lubrication, and safety testing. We provide prompt hurricane preparation before storm season. When repairs are needed, our technicians arrive equipped with premium parts and complete most calls in a single visit.
