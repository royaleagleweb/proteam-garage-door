Residential Garage Door Styles

Find the perfect garage door style to complement your home's architecture and boost your curb appeal.

Explore Our Garage Door Styles

From sleek modern designs to timeless classics, we carry garage door styles to suit every South Florida home. Browse our collection and find the look that's right for you.

Modern & Contemporary

Clean lines, aluminum frames with frosted or clear glass panels, and minimalist design define this popular style. Perfect for contemporary South Florida homes looking for a sleek, updated facade. Choose from Modern Steel, full-view aluminum, and aluminum-and-glass options.

Traditional Raised Panel

The classic raised-panel garage door remains America's most popular style for good reason. Available in steel or wood construction with multiple color options, decorative window inserts, and insulation levels to match any traditional home exterior.

Carriage House

Capture old-world charm without sacrificing modern performance. Steel construction with decorative strap hinges and handles creates the classic swing-out look while operating on a standard overhead track system. A favorite in upscale neighborhoods.

Mediterranean

Arched windows, wrought-iron hardware, and warm wood tones designed to complement the Spanish and Mediterranean architecture common throughout South Florida. These doors bring authentic character to stucco and tile-roof homes.

Wood-Look

Faux wood composite overlays applied to steel doors give you the beauty of real wood grain without the warping, cracking, or constant maintenance. Hurricane-rated options are available, making this style practical and stunning for coastal living.

Custom

One-of-a-kind designs tailored to your exact specifications. Custom sizes, materials, colors, and window configurations let you create a garage door that's as unique as your home. Ideal for architecturally distinctive properties.

Impact Glass

Full-view aluminum and glass doors featuring hurricane-rated impact glass. Maximize natural light and deliver striking modern curb appeal while meeting South Florida's stringent wind-load requirements. Form and function in one elegant package.

Flush Panel

Smooth, flat surfaces with clean aesthetics make flush panel doors a top pick for modern and minimalist homes. Available in steel or aluminum with optional insulation, these doors offer a contemporary look that pairs well with flat-roof architecture.

Hurricane-Rated Options

Every style in our gallery is available with wind-load certification engineered for South Florida's hurricane season. Our hurricane-rated garage doors meet Florida Building Code and Miami-Dade HVHZ (High-Velocity Hurricane Zone) standards, so you never have to compromise between the look you want and the protection your home needs. We handle the permit paperwork and NOA documentation for you.
