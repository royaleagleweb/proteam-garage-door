Garage Door Parts & Accessories

Remotes, keypads, springs, smart home modules, and everything you need to keep your garage door running smoothly.

Remotes & Keypads

ProTeam Garage Door stocks a full line of garage door remotes, wireless keypads, and smart control devices from LiftMaster, Chamberlain, Genie, and other leading brands. Whether you need a simple replacement clicker or a full smart-home upgrade, we carry the right product and can program it on the spot.

  • Wireless Keypads — Featuring Security+ 2.0 rolling code technology for maximum protection against code theft. Mount outside your garage for keyless entry with a personal PIN.
  • Multi-Button Transmitters — Control up to 3 garage doors or gates from a single remote. Ideal for households with multiple openers or a separate gate.
  • Mini Remotes — Compact key-chain remotes that clip onto your keys for convenient, on-the-go access without a bulky visor remote.
  • Universal Transmitters — Compatible with multiple garage door opener brands, so you can consolidate remotes even if your doors use different systems.
  • Internet Gateways & WiFi Modules — Connect your opener to your home network and control it from anywhere via the myQ smartphone app.

Replacement Parts

We keep thousands of replacement parts in stock for same-day repairs across Broward and Palm Beach County. Our technicians only install manufacturer-grade components to ensure long-lasting performance and safe operation.

  • Springs — Torsion and extension springs in standard and high-cycle options. We match the exact wire gauge, length, and inside diameter your door requires.
  • Cables & Cable Drums — Aircraft-grade galvanized cables and precision-machined drums for reliable lifting every cycle.
  • Rollers — Nylon rollers for whisper-quiet operation or steel rollers for heavy-duty commercial applications. Both available in 10-ball-bearing and 13-ball-bearing configurations.
  • Hinges & Brackets — Heavy-gauge steel hinges, end brackets, and center brackets that maintain proper panel alignment under load.
  • Weather Seals & Bottom Seals — Flexible vinyl and rubber seals that block rain, dust, insects, and drafts. Essential for South Florida's storm season.
  • Track & Hardware — Vertical and horizontal track sections, flag brackets, angle iron, and fasteners for complete track assemblies.
  • Panels — Individual replacement sections to restore a damaged door without a full replacement. Available in matching colors, styles, and insulation values.

Smart Home Integration

Turn your garage door into a connected device with smart home accessories from LiftMaster and Chamberlain. Monitor and control your door from anywhere in the world, set schedules, and receive instant alerts — all from your phone.

  • myQ App Compatibility — Works with most LiftMaster and Chamberlain openers manufactured after 1998. Free app download with no monthly subscription fees.
  • Voice Assistant Support — Integrates with Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, and Apple HomeKit so you can open, close, or check your door with a voice command.
  • Real-Time Alerts — Get push notifications on your phone every time the garage door opens or closes, so you always know who is coming and going.
  • Guest Access & Scheduling — Grant temporary access to dog walkers, delivery drivers, or housekeepers and set schedules to auto-close the door at night.
  • Built-In Camera Options — The LiftMaster Secure View opener features an integrated camera with live-stream video, two-way audio, and motion-activated recording.
